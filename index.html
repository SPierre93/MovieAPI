Information

As we re-open our operations, we encourage our patrons to remain cautious now that quarantine has been lifted. To curb the spread of COVID-19, the Management and Staff of Spotlight Cinemas ask that you follow the new regulations outlined below for the safety and protection of you, our Valued Customers, as well as that of our Team.


These guidelines are in-keeping with the Ministry of Health reccomendations.

Free-roaming

Everyone must wear a mask that covers both the nose and mouth to gain entry to the premises. Masks must stay on at all times unless eating or drinking.


Use the sanitation stations available at various checkpoints for access to hand sanitizer and washing basins.


Stand on the red, circular stickers that have been placed on the ground whenever you are expected to stand in lines.

In-theatre

All patrons are required to sit one seat apart. Ushers will be available to ensure that everyone is able to be seated regardless of the new policy.


You may take your seats 20 minutes before the movie begins, but no earlier as preparations must be made during this time.


Please leave promptly once the movie ends so that the theatre can be properly sanitized afterward. Patrons may view end credit scenes if applicable.

Coney Island

All rides will operate at half-capacity: no one will be alowed to sit beside another on any ride.


There will be a longer wait than usual to accomodate sanitization between each batch of patrons utlising the rides.


Do not move the chairs around in the Food Court area as they have been placed strategically to maintain social distancing. There will no longer be more than four persons per table at this time.
